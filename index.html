Where the benefits administration industry gets read straight.

COBRA and state continuation coverage sit at the intersection of federal law, state insurance codes, and whatever a given TPA's operations happen to look like that quarter. Most of what gets written about it is either a law firm's compliance alert or a vendor's sales page.

Custodian is neither. It's a running account of what's actually changing in regulation, in the industry's technology and consolidation, and in the day-to-day mechanics of running cases — read by the people doing the work, not selling around it.
