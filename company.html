Kelly Benefits — The Full Circle

A privately-held, family-owned benefits company built around four connected divisions — each one sells into (or draws from) the next, forming a closed loop of the client relationship rather than a single-service business.

Founded
1976
Structure

Other offices

One client relationshipSold, administered, paid, and invested — through a shared Total Benefits Solution® platform.

Why the circle matters — each division individually competes against specialists that are often dramatically larger — Gallagher in consulting, WEX/Alight in administration, ADP/Paychex in payroll, CAPTRUST/OneDigital in retirement. Almost none of those specialists connect all four under one client relationship and one technology platform the way Kelly does. If there's a strategic edge here, it's integration, not any single division's scale — and OneDigital (health + wealth convergence) is the one competitor pursuing the same idea, just at a much larger scale.
